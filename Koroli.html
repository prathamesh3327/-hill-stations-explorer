Description

Koroli is a lesser-known hill station in Maharashtra, known for its unspoiled natural beauty and tranquility. It is an excellent destination for a quiet and relaxing getaway, offering lush green landscapes, serene environment, and a chance to experience the untouched beauty of nature.

How to Get There

Koroli is approximately 150 kilometers from Mumbai and around 120 kilometers from Pune. The hill station is accessible by road and can be reached by car or bus from both cities. The journey to Koroli is scenic, passing through picturesque landscapes and small villages.

  • By Air: The nearest airport is Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport in Mumbai, from where you can hire a taxi or take a bus to Koroli.
  • By Rail: The nearest railway station is Igatpuri Railway Station, which is around 60 kilometers away. From Igatpuri, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to Koroli.
  • By Road: Koroli is well-connected by road and can be accessed via the Mumbai-Nashik Highway (NH-3) and then local roads leading to the hill station.
