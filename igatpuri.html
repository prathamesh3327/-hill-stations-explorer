Description

Igatpuri is a scenic hill station located in the Western Ghats of Maharashtra. Famous for its lush greenery, pleasant climate, and stunning waterfalls, Igatpuri is a popular destination for nature lovers and trekkers. It is also home to the Vipassana International Academy, where people come for meditation and spiritual retreats.

How to Get There

Igatpuri is approximately 130 kilometers from Mumbai and about 50 kilometers from Nashik. The hill station is well-connected by road and can be reached by car or bus from both cities. The journey offers breathtaking views of the Western Ghats.

  • By Air: The nearest airport is Mumbai International Airport, from where you can hire a taxi or take a bus to Igatpuri.
  • By Rail: Igatpuri has its own railway station, which is a major stop on the Mumbai-Nashik route. Many express and local trains stop at Igatpuri.
  • By Road: Igatpuri is well-connected by road, and you can drive via the Mumbai-Nashik Highway (NH-3) or take a state transport bus from Mumbai or Nashik.
