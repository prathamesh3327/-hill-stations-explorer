Description

Lonavala is a renowned hill station in the Western Ghats of Maharashtra. Famous for its scenic views, lush greenery, and waterfalls, Lonavala is a popular destination for tourists seeking a refreshing escape from city life. It offers numerous trekking trails, caves, and beautiful lakes, making it a favorite spot for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

How to Get There

Lonavala is approximately 83 kilometers from Mumbai and around 65 kilometers from Pune. The hill station is well-connected by road and can be easily reached by car or bus from both cities. The Mumbai-Pune Expressway (NH-48) offers a quick and scenic route to Lonavala.

  • By Air: The nearest airport is Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport in Mumbai, from where you can hire a taxi or take a bus to Lonavala.
  • By Rail: Lonavala has its own railway station, which is a major stop on the Mumbai-Pune route. Several express and local trains connect Lonavala to Mumbai and Pune.
  • By Road: Lonavala is well-connected by road and can be accessed via the Mumbai-Pune Expressway (NH-48) and the Lonavala Road.
