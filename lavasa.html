Lavasa - A Serene Hill Station

Description

Lavasa is a planned city near Pune in Maharashtra, known for its beautiful landscapes and serene environment. Modeled after the Italian town of Portofino, Lavasa is spread across seven hills, covering an area of 25,000 acres. It offers a perfect retreat for those seeking peace and tranquility amidst nature.

How to Get There

Lavasa is approximately 60 kilometers from Pune and around 180 kilometers from Mumbai. The best way to reach Lavasa is by road. You can hire a taxi or drive your own vehicle from Pune or Mumbai. The route offers picturesque views and a pleasant drive.

  • By Air: The nearest airport is Pune International Airport, which is well-connected to major cities in India.
  • By Rail: The nearest railway station is Pune Railway Station, from where you can hire a cab or take a bus to Lavasa.
  • By Road: Lavasa is well-connected by road and can be reached via the Mumbai-Pune Expressway and then the Lavasa Road.
