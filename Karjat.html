Description

Karjat is a scenic hill station located near Mumbai in Maharashtra. Known for its lush greenery, waterfalls, and picturesque landscapes, Karjat is a popular destination for trekkers, adventure enthusiasts, and nature lovers. It offers various trekking trails, historical sites, and farm stays, making it an ideal getaway from the bustling city life of Mumbai.

How to Get There

Karjat is approximately 70 kilometers from Mumbai and around 100 kilometers from Pune. The hill station is well-connected by road and can be easily reached by car or bus from both cities. The journey to Karjat is scenic, passing through lush green landscapes and small villages.

  • By Air: The nearest airport is Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport in Mumbai, from where you can hire a taxi or take a bus to Karjat.
  • By Rail: Karjat has its own railway station, which is well-connected to Mumbai and Pune via local and express trains.
  • By Road: Karjat is well-connected by road and can be accessed via the Mumbai-Pune Expressway (NH-48) and then the Karjat-Murbad Road.
