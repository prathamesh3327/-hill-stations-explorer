Description

Jawhar is a picturesque hill station located in the Palghar district of Maharashtra. Known for its scenic beauty, cultural heritage, and ancient forts, Jawhar offers a tranquil retreat for nature lovers and history enthusiasts alike. The region is surrounded by lush green hills, waterfalls, and tribal villages, making it a unique destination in Maharashtra.

How to Get There

Jawhar is approximately 180 kilometers from Mumbai and about 80 kilometers from Nashik. The hill station is well-connected by road and can be reached by car or bus from both cities. The journey to Jawhar is scenic, with winding roads and beautiful landscapes along the way.

  • By Air: The nearest airport is Mumbai International Airport, from where you can hire a taxi or take a bus to Jawhar.
  • By Rail: The nearest railway station is Igatpuri Railway Station, which is around 60 kilometers away. From Igatpuri, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to Jawhar.
  • By Road: Jawhar is well-connected by road, and you can drive via the Mumbai-Nashik Highway (NH-3) or take a state transport bus from Mumbai or Nashik.
